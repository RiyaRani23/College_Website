About UCET

Excellence in Engineering Education Since 2009

Welcome to UCET Hazaribagh

The University College of Engineering and Technology (UCET) in Hazaribagh is a government engineering college established in 2009 and affiliated with Vinoba Bhave University (VBU). Recognized by the UGC and accredited by NAAC, UCET offers B.Tech courses with admissions based on JEE Main or JCECE scores. The college is located on a 67.17-acre campus and provides hostel accommodation and a cafeteria for its students.

Key Information

Name: University College of Engineering and Technology (UCET), Hazaribagh
Affiliation: Affiliated with Vinoba Bhave University (VBU), Hazaribagh
Establishment: 2009
Recognition: Approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited by NAAC with a 'B++' grade
Campus: Situated on a 67.17-acre residential campus, offering hostel facilities and a cafeteria

Courses and Admissions

Programs: Primarily offers B.Tech courses
Admissions: Admission to the B.Tech program is through the JEE Main or JCECE entrance exams

Placements

UCET has a notable placement record, with companies such as TCS and Vedanta recruiting from the college. In the 2023-24 academic year, the placement rate was around 56%.

"Welcome to UCET Hazaribagh, where innovation meets excellence!"

Greetings and warm welcome to all at University College of Engineering and Technology Hazaribagh, India. UCET Hazaribagh is one of the premier autonomous institutions of national importance under the aegis of the Ministry of Education, Government of India.

Our institution was established in 2010 with state-of-the-art facilities in Science and Technology. We are recognized nationally and internationally for excellent education at the undergraduate and postgraduate levels, while making significant strides in innovative research and development activities across various domains.

At UCET Hazaribagh, we believe in nurturing not just technical skills but also leadership qualities, ethical values, and social responsibility. Our dedicated faculty, modern infrastructure, and industry partnerships ensure that our students are well-prepared for the challenges of tomorrow.
